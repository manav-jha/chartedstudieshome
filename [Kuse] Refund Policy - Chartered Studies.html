Refund Policy

At Chartered Studies, we value your trust. Our refund policy is designed to be transparent and fair for all our professional learners.

Last Updated: October 2023 Effective Date: Immediate

1. Eligibility for Refund

Refunds are applicable only under the following specific conditions:

  • Technical issues preventing access to the course content that cannot be resolved by our support team within 48 hours.
  • Accidental double payment for the same course or subscription.
  • Content not matching the course description provided at the time of purchase.
  • Cancellation of a live batch or session by Chartered Studies.

Note: Refunds are NOT applicable if more than 10% of the course content has been consumed or if the study materials/books have already been dispatched.

2. Refund Period

Refund requests must be initiated within 7 days of the original purchase date. Requests made after this 7-day window will not be entertained under any circumstances, except in cases of batch cancellation by the institute.

3. Processing Time

Review Process

Our accounts team will review your request and verify eligibility within 2-3 working days.

Credit Window

Once approved, the refund amount will be credited to your original payment method within 5-7 working days.
