Shipping & Delivery Policy

Detailed information about how we deliver our professional study materials and digital content to you.

Dispatch Time

At Chartered Studies, we understand the urgency of exam preparation. For our physical products, including the Test Series Programme and study modules:

  • Orders are typically processed and dispatched within 24 to 48 working hours of order confirmation.
  • Orders placed on weekends or public holidays will be processed on the next business day.
  • You will receive an email and SMS notification once your package has been handed over to our courier partner.

Delivery Estimates

Metros & Tier 1 Cities

3 - 5 Business Days

Rest of India

5 - 7 Business Days

*Please note that delivery times may vary due to external factors like weather conditions, logistics strikes, or remote locations in Bihar and other states.

Tracking Information

Transparency is key to our student community's trust. Once your order is dispatched:

1

A unique Tracking ID will be sent to your registered email address and mobile number.

2

You can track your parcel directly on our courier partner's website using the link provided in the notification.

3

If you encounter any issues with tracking, please contact us at [email protected].

Digital Content Access

Instant Delivery:

Access to online courses and recorded lectures is typically granted immediately upon successful payment processing.

Access Method:

Check your "My Courses" dashboard or your email for login credentials and activation keys. Please ensure you check your 'Spam' or 'Promotions' folder if you don't see the email in your Inbox.
