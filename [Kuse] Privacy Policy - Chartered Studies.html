01 Introduction

Welcome to Chartered Studies ("we," "our," or "us"). As India's most trusted EdTech for professionals, we recognize the importance of privacy and transparency. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you visit our website or use our educational services.

By accessing our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We ensure that your data is handled securely and in compliance with the relevant data protection laws in India.

02 Data Collection

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our service to you:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, phone number, and educational background provided during registration.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you access the website, including IP addresses, browser types, and pages visited.
  • Transaction Data: Details about payments and subscriptions (processed via secure third-party gateways).

03 How We Use Data

Chartered Studies uses the collected data for various purposes:

Service Delivery

To provide access to courses, study materials, and track your learning progress.

Communication

To notify you about updates, course availability, and respond to support queries.

Improvement

To analyze usage patterns and improve the user interface and educational content.

Legal Compliance

To fulfill accounting requirements and comply with applicable laws.

04 Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With third-party vendors who assist in payment processing, data analysis, or email delivery.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.
  • Business Transfer: In connection with any merger, sale of company assets, or acquisition.

05 Cookies Policy

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our service and hold certain information. Cookies are files with small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier.

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use some portions of our Service.

06 Data Security

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, including SSL encryption for data transfer and restricted access to database servers. Our team regularly reviews our security protocols to ensure your data remains protected against unauthorized access.
